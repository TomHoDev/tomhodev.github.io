Ash Ketchum


image source

Ash Ketchum is the protagonist of the Pokemon Storyline. The games that are part of the Pokemon franchise focus on the player being part of the Pokemon world. The manga and anime follow Ash. His dream is to become a Pokemon Master.

His Pokemon in the first generation were :

  • Pikachu
  • Butterfree (evolved from a captured Caterpie and eventually released)
  • Pidgeot (evolved from a captured Pidgeotto and eventually released)
  • Bulbasaur
  • Charizard (evolved from a captured Charmander)
  • Squirtle
  • Raticate
  • Kingler (evolved from a captured Krabby)
  • Primeape
  • Muk
  • Taurus
  • Snorlax
  • Lapras (eventually released)

Misty


image source

Misty is a companion of Ash. She joins him after he steals her bike in an attempt to save his Pokemon and friend Pikachu. The bike is eventually destroyed so Misty decides to stick around Ash until he is bale to repay his debt. Misty is an aspiring Water type Pokemon Trainer and the gym leader of Cerulean City, her hometown

Her Pokemon in the first generation were :

  • Staryu
  • Starmie
  • Goldeen
  • Psyduck

Brock


image source

Brock is another companion of Ash. He joins Ash after Ash is able to beat Brock in a Pokemon battle. Brock was the gym leader of Pewter City. Brock left his family in order to become a world class Pokemon Breeder

His Pokemon in the first generation were :

  • Onix
  • Geodude
  • Zubat
  • Vulpix
