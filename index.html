What are Pokemon?

Pokemon are fictional beings similar to animals in the Pokemon franchise created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995. Pokemon have special abilities that include generation or control of the elements. Pokemon can be wild or may have partenered with Pokemon Trainers.
